FAQ — Aurora Group Benefits Insurance

Q: Can I have both group and individual life insurance?

A: Yes. They complement each other. Group insurance is a foundation; individual insurance fills the coverage gap.

Q: What happens to my group coverage if I leave my Aurora job?

A: Most group life policies have a conversion option within 31 days. The new policy is typically more expensive due to conversion rates with no underwriting.

Q: Does my Aurora employer have to offer life insurance?

A: No. There is no federal requirement to offer life insurance. Some states have specific requirements, but Colorado does not mandate group life coverage.
