Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What's the minimum group size for group benefits in Reno?

Most carriers require 10 eligible employees for fully underwritten group term life. However, for smaller employers (2–9 lives), worksite voluntary benefits and association health plans can provide similar coverage at competitive rates. The Nevada Department of Insurance can provide a list of carriers writing small-group business in Washoe County.

Q: Are group life benefits taxable to employees?

The first $50,000 of employer-paid group term life is tax-free to the employee under IRS Section 79. Coverage above $50,000 generates modest imputed income based on IRS Table I rates. For a 40-year-old Reno technician earning $55,000 with 1x salary group life, there is no tax impact.

Q: Can employees keep their group life insurance if they leave their job?

Standard group term life ends when employment ends. However, most carriers offer conversion or portability options — employees can convert group coverage to an individual policy without evidence of insurability or take a portable term policy with them. This is a valuable retention feature to highlight during open enrollment.
