Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does Ohio have a state estate tax?

A: No. Ohio fully repealed its state estate tax effective 2013. The federal $13.99M exemption still applies.

Q: How does whole life fund a buy-sell agreement?

A: Each owner buys a whole life policy on the other owners. At death, the business receives the death benefit tax-free and buys the deceased's shares.

Q: What coverage should a Cleveland couple consider?

A: $100k–$250k per spouse is common. For family businesses, $500k–$1M per owner is typical.
