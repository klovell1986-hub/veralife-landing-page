FAQ — Henderson Final Expense Insurance

Q: What if I have pre-existing conditions?

A: Final expense policies are designed for people with health conditions. Most carriers accept well-managed diabetes, high blood pressure, history of cancer (5+ years remission), and heart conditions.

Q: Is the death benefit taxable?

A: No. Life insurance death benefits are paid income tax-free to your beneficiary under IRC Section 101(a).

Q: Can I apply for a policy for my aging parent?

A: Yes. Many adult children in Henderson help their parents apply for final expense coverage. You can be the applicant with your parent as the insured, provided you have insurable interest.

Q: What's the difference between final expense and term life?

A: Final expense is a whole life policy — premiums never increase, coverage never expires. Term life covers you for a set period (10–30 years) at a lower initial cost.
