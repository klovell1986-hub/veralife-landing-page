With a $2M term policy ($200–$280/month for a 40-year-old):

FAQ — Plano Term Life Insurance

Q: Is group life through my corporate job enough?

A: For top earners, rarely. Employer coverage (1–2× salary) replaces too little income. A personal term policy fills the gap and stays with you if you change jobs.

Q: Should I use bonus buy-up programs at work?

A: Supplemental coverage through work can be convenient but is often more expensive than individual coverage for healthy professionals, and still ends when you leave the job.

Q: Can I get a policy that covers both me and my spouse?

A: Yes. Joint first-to-die policies are available, but two individual policies are usually more flexible and cost-efficient.
