Q: What NJ-specific mandates apply?

A: NJ requires TDI through the state or a private plan. NJ COBRA applies to employers with 2–19 employees.

Q: Can small businesses afford group benefits?

A: Basic group life starts at $1.50 PEPM. For a 10-employee firm, total cost is approximately $50–$100/month.

Q: What minimum employee count is required?

A: Most carriers offer group life to businesses with as few as 2–3 eligible employees in New Jersey. Guaranteed-issue (no health questions) is standard at this size, though benefit amounts may be capped at $25k–$50k per employee.

This is especially valuable in Newark's biotech and logistics sectors where employees want higher coverage than the employer's base benefit.
