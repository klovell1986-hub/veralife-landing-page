FAQ — Sandy Springs Group Benefits

Q: How much does group life insurance cost a Sandy Springs employer?

A: For a company of 20 employees: basic group term life costs $15–$30/employee/month. LTD adds $10–$20/employee/month. Group life (up to $50k) is typically tax-free to employees.

Q: What's the difference between group and voluntary benefits?

A: Group benefits are employer-paid (or employer-subsidized). Voluntary benefits are fully employee-paid through payroll deduction, but the employer enables access to group pricing.
